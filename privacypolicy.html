Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what c00lkiddtech collects and why. By using the site, you agree to this policy.

1. Jetstream email and search logging

When you sign in to Jetstream with Google, we receive and store your Google account email in a signed session cookie so you can stay signed in.

When you search or browse through Jetstream, we log:

  • Your Google account email
  • What you typed or searched
  • The destination URL you were sent to
  • Your IP address
  • Your user-agent string

We log this to monitor for NSFW content, illegal content, and abuse. This helps protect the site, users, and schools from liability if someone misuses Jetstream on a school network.

If you use Jetstream to search for or access prohibited content, your email may be added to a Jetstream blacklist. Blacklisted emails cannot sign in to Jetstream until we remove the block.

2. IP addresses

We collect IP addresses when you visit pages on the site and when you use Jetstream.

We use IP addresses to:

  • Detect bots, scrapers, and automated abuse
  • Identify possible DDoS or flooding attempts
  • Apply IP bans when needed
  • Investigate security issues and rule violations

Banned IPs are stored in our ban list and blocked from accessing the website.

3. User-agent strings

We collect your browser user-agent on visits and Jetstream activity.

We use user-agents to:

  • Tell real browsers apart from bots and scripts
  • Spot suspicious or automated traffic patterns
  • Help debug browser-specific issues when something breaks
  • Support abuse and security reviews alongside IP logging

4. Site visit logging

For general page visits, we may log your IP address, the page path you requested, and your user-agent. Visit logs are rate-limited so we do not log every single asset request. Static files like images, CSS, and JavaScript are usually not logged.

5. Cookies

Jetstream uses a session cookie after Google sign-in so you stay logged in. You can clear cookies in your browser to sign out. Other site features may use local storage for settings such as background color.

6. Third parties

Google handles Jetstream sign-in and has its own privacy policy. Games and ads may be served by third parties that collect data under their own policies. We do not control third-party tracking on external sites you open through Jetstream or game embeds.

7. How long we keep data

Session data lasts for the life of the session cookie. Logs sent to our moderation webhooks are kept as long as needed for safety, abuse review, and legal protection. IP bans remain until removed by an administrator.

8. Children and schools

If you are using a school network, your school may also monitor network activity. Do not use Jetstream for NSFW, illegal, or prohibited searches. Violations may result in email blacklist, IP ban, or reports to moderators.

9. Your choices

You can avoid Jetstream logging by not signing in to Jetstream. You can clear cookies to end your session. If you believe you were banned by mistake, contact us through Fluxer.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.
