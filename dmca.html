DMCA

Last updated: June 16, 2026

c00lkiddtech respects intellectual property rights. If you believe content on this site infringes your copyright, you may send a DMCA notice with the information below.

What to include

Your notice should include:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Identification of the copyrighted work you believe was infringed
  • Identification of the material on c00lkiddtech that you want removed, with enough detail for us to find it (URL, page, game name, etc.)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the owner's behalf
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Counter-notification

If you believe content was removed by mistake, you may send a counter-notification that includes the information required under the DMCA, including consent to jurisdiction and service of process where applicable.

Repeat infringers

We may terminate or restrict access for users who repeatedly infringe copyrights or related rights.
